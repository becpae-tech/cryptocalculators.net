About Dai (DAI)

Dai (DAI) is a decentralized stablecoin on the Ethereum blockchain, pegged to the US Dollar (1 DAI ≈ 1 USD). DAI is designed to maintain a stable value while providing the benefits of cryptocurrency.

  • Launch Date: 2017
  • Purpose: Stable digital currency for DeFi, payments, and trading
  • Key Features: Dollar-pegged, decentralized, transparent, and Ethereum-based
  • Use Cases: Digital payments, lending, borrowing, staking, and decentralized finance applications
