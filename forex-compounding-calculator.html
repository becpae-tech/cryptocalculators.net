What is a forex compounding calculator?

A forex compounding calculator is a tool used by traders to figure out the future value of a forex trade over time. It shows the effect of reinvesting capital and interest, providing exponential growth insights displayed in numbers and a yield chart. Results are indicative and cannot guarantee profit.

How does it work?

The calculator applies the power of compound interest, reinvesting gains over each period according to the selected compounding frequency. Inputs needed include initial investment, interest rate, time, compounding frequency, additional contributions, and contribution frequency. This projects growth over time.

How to calculate compound interest in forex

Compound interest considers both the initial investment and accumulated gains. Compounding can be done monthly, quarterly, or yearly, with optional additional contributions. The standard formula is:

A = P (1 + r/n)^(n*t), where A=future value, P=principal, r=annual rate, n=compounds/year, t=time in years.
