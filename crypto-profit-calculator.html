How to use the Crypto Profit Calculator
  • Enter your investment amount in the “Investment ($)” field.
  • Enter the buy price in “Buy Price (USD)”.
  • Enter the sell price in “Sell Price (USD)”.
  • Optional: Enter fees for investment and withdrawal, and select fee type (% or $).
  • Click “Calculate Profit” to see Total Profit/Loss, ROI, Break-Even Price per coin, and Total Exit Amount.
  • Profits are never guaranteed, even if calculated in advance.
How to use the Crypto Investment Calculator
  • Enter the initial investment ($).
  • Enter additional contributions ($).
  • Select contribution frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly).
  • Enter expected annual return (%).
  • Enter investment duration in years.
  • Click “Calculate Investment” to see Future Value, Total Contributions, Total Profit, and CAGR.
What is a Crypto Profit Calculator?
  • A tool to calculate potential profit or loss from a crypto trade.
  • Allows input of buy price, sell price, number of coins, and optional fees.
  • Shows results including Total Profit/Loss, ROI, Break-Even Price, and Total Exit Amount.
How does it work?
  • Calculates total cost including purchase price and fees.
  • Computes revenue from selling price and coin amount.
  • Net profit = Revenue − Total Cost.
  • ROI (%) = (Profit ÷ Total Cost) × 100.
  • Break-Even Price = Total Cost ÷ Number of Coins.
Crypto Profit Formula
  • Profit = (Sell Price × Number of Coins) − Total Cost
  • Total Cost includes: Buy Price × Number of Coins + fees
  • ROI (%) = (Profit ÷ Total Cost) × 100
  • Break-Even Price = Total Cost ÷ Number of Coins
