Crypto Market Cap Calculator

Use our crypto market cap calculator to find out the market capitalization of a cryptocurrency or add several coins simultaneously to compare their market caps.

Information: Use the calculator to find out the market cap of your favorite cryptocurrency or combine many to find out the total market cap of a group of coins.

How to use the crypto market cap calculator

  • Add the ‘Crypto Price’.
  • Enter the ‘Circulating Supply’.
  • The calculation is made automatically and will show next to “Market Cap:”.
  • The value will also be shown under the “Total Market Cap”.
  • Click ‘Add More’ to add new rows for additional coins.
  • Each new coin’s market cap is calculated automatically and added to total.
  • Ranking shows highest market cap first.
  • Remove any coin with “✖” button.

Why use this calculator?

It helps investors find out how valuable a cryptocurrency is and compare multiple projects quickly. Results are hypothetical and not investment advice.

Formula and market cap explained

Market cap = crypto price × circulating supply

  • Crypto price: Current price of one unit of the cryptocurrency.
  • Circulating supply: Total number of units currently in circulation.
