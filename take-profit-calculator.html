Take Profit Calculator

Calculate your take profit levels, target prices, and potential profit for crypto, forex, or stock trades. Add multiple levels to scale out positions.

Take Profit Calculator
+ Add Take Profit Level
Information: The take profit calculator calculates your exit prices and projected outcomes (actual results vary with market conditions) based on your entry price, position size, and profit targets. Add multiple take profit levels to scale out your positions.
How to use the Take Profit Calculator
  1. Enter your Entry Price (e.g., $1.04534).
  2. Enter your Position Value (e.g., 1000).
  3. Select your currency.
  4. Choose ‘Long’ or ‘Short’ position.
  5. Add one or more Profit Target Levels (%) and click Calculate.
Take Profit Calculator Formula / Calculation

Long Trade: Target Price = Entry Price × (1 + Profit Target % / 100)
Short Trade: Target Price = Entry Price × (1 − Profit Target % / 100)
Profit = Position Value × (Profit Target % / 100)

Example

Entry Price: 1.00, Position: 1000 USD, Long Trade, Profit Level 5%

Target Price = 1 × (1 + 0.05) = 1.05
Profit = 1000 × 0.05 = 50 USD
